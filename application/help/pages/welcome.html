Welcome

De MonsterDebugger is a lightweight debugger for Adobe Flex, Flash and AIR projects that is made and maintained by Amsterdam based design studio De Monsters.

With De MonsterDebugger you can:
  • Explore your application, adjust properties and run methods at runtime.
  • Trace Strings, Arrays, Objects or even custom classes to De MonsterDebugger and it will show you the entire structure or that specific object.
  • Highlight DisplayObjects while you're exploring the live application.
  • Receive completely free auto updates when we've fixed a bug or implemented a new feature.
  • Do all this with projects that run live in a browser or even on the desktop.
